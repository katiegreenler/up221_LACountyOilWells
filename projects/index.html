Welcome to the Projects Page!

This is a sample sub-page located at /projects/index.html.

You can access this page at whatever.com/projects/.

If you create another directory within the projects folder with its own index.html file, it will act as another sub-page.

For example, /projects/projects1/index.html would be accessible at whatever.com/projects/project1/.

Feel free to customize this page as needed for your project!
